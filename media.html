Brand New Website of Govt Girls Polytechnic, Gorakhpur launched on 15th Sepetember 2023

Posted on

NOVEMBER 7, 2023september 15, 2023


A brand New Website of Government Girl’s Polytechnic Gorakhpur launched on 14th Oct 2023.

It is developed by Cyberpassion and has online Grievance Redressal System for Student, Employee and Parents, feeback form, details of employees, location on Google map and lot of other things.

We are happy to announce that website with online grievance redressal system for Government Girl’s Polytechnic Gorakhpur was successfully launched on 14th Oct 2022. The website as well as Online Grievance Redressal System was developed by Cyberpassion which is a startup based in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh. The company specializes in developing online cloud based systems for educational institutes, hospitals, clinic as well as Android and iOS apps for businesses. The company has more than 250 clients across pan India.
